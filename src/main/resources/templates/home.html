Welcome!

Someday I Will application is my newest project. The main feature is storing information about books you want to read, and movies you want to watch in the future. To start creating lists you have to signup and login. You can search for books/movies and add them to your lists, but you can also provide all the necessary information on your own.

Visit the SwaggerUI page to learn more about this application. You will find there information about all models and available endpoints.
